Spätzle mit Soß

Description

Spätzle mit Soß is easy to make. Find out by yourself!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Add Flour
  2. Add Eggs
  3. Add Salt
  4. Stir until you have Dough
  5. Put Dough in Press and press it in boiling Water
  6. Cook it for a few Minutes
  7. Make Sauce. Find out by yourself
  8. Enjoy!
